地名 — Regional office names

The place name printed at the top of a Japanese license plate, mapped to the 運輸支局 / 自動車検査登録事務所 that issues it. Some chimei (e.g. 知床, 富士山) are assigned to more than one office.

分類番号 — Classification numbers

The 3-digit (or 2-digit legacy) number above the hiragana, identifying the vehicle class.

ひらがな — Hiragana usage

Of the 46 hiragana in the gojūon, only 40 appear on plates. Six are excluded for visual, phonetic, or semantic reasons — hover an excluded character to see why.
